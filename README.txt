Cents: put sense into your finances 

Hello! We are very happy you decided to have a look at our project!
We created this simple mobile budgeting app to help you stay on top of your finnces and track your spending.
We used React Native and Redux to build this app as part of our +Masters course with Code First Girls.
This app is our final assement for the course.

Features:
inspirational quote
budget input 
multiple accounts
spending tracker - how much of your budget is gone

What do I do with this project:

clone the repository into the desired folder
using XCODE simulator feature on Mac or any simulator of your choice open the app and explore it
send us fededback and ideas for improvement
